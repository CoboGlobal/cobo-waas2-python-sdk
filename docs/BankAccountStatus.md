# BankAccountStatus

BankAccountStatus defines the status of the bank account: - `Pending`: The bank account is pending verification by Cobo. - `Verified`: The bank account has been verified by Cobo. - `Rejected`: The bank account has been rejected by Cobo. - `Deleted`: The bank account has been deleted.
